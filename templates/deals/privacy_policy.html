Our website contains affiliate links to third-party stores (Amazon, Flipkart, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these websites. Please review their privacy policies before making purchases.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share anonymized data with analytics providers.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights
